Carbon Footprint

The carbon footprint is the total amount of greenhouse gases, especially carbon dioxide, that are emitted directly or indirectly by human activities. It includes the emissions from transportation, electricity usage, food production, and manufacturing. Reducing our carbon footprint helps to slow down global warming and fight climate change.
